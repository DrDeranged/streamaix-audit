⚙️ PART 1: ADDITIONAL FEATURES TO BUILD

These add deeper functionality, interactivity, and user retention.

⸻

🧠 1. “Summarize a Link” Input Module

Let users paste a YouTube, Spotify, or Rumble link and generate a live AI summary.

Features:
	•	Auto-detect platform
	•	Show loading progress bar (transcription → summarization → formatting)
	•	Output: Blog-style summary with toggle for TL;DR, timeline, quotes
	•	Optional: Auto-publish to IPFS or Arweave with one click

⸻

🧬 2. AI Remix Generator

Turn any summary into different tones or styles

Modes:
	•	🧵 Twitter thread
	•	🤓 Academic breakdown
	•	🧠 Naval-style philosophy
	•	💬 Gen-Z meme talk

Interactive UI:
	•	Dropdown or emoji selector
	•	Button: “Remix Summary”

⸻

📡 3. “Trending on Farcaster” Feed

Surface summaries based on what’s hot on social Web3

How:
	•	Scrape Farcaster channels for most liked/shared podcast/video links
	•	Auto-summarize → add to a public “trending” board
	•	Users can upvote, tip, or follow related content

⸻

📖 4. Knowledge Stacks

Let users group summaries into “knowledge playlists” like:

	•	“AI & Ethics”
	•	“Crypto Regulation in 2025”
	•	“ZK Rollups Deep Dive”

Each stack:
	•	Can be public or private
	•	Has a unique URL (like a Notion folder)
	•	Can be minted as a collectible (Knowledge NFTs)

⸻

🧑‍🚀 5. AI Companion Agent

A personal AI that learns your preferences over time.

You can:
	•	Ask it: “What should I learn today?”
	•	Auto-summarize weekly Farcaster content you follow
	•	Sync it with your ENS identity or Lens profile

⸻

🏆 6. Leaderboard & Social Clout

Highlight top contributors, readers, and curators.

Metrics:
	•	Most liked summaries
	•	Most tipped curator
	•	Most followed “Knowledge Avatar”
	•	Top remixes

Gamify it with badges or SBTs.

⸻

🧪 7. Summary Verification DAO

Allow the community to “audit” AI outputs for accuracy.

Flow:
	1.	AI summary is created
	2.	Users can flag or suggest improvements
	3.	Verified summary gets a ✅ badge
	4.	Contributors share tips or bounty
